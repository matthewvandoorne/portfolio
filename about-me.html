About me


My name is Matthew van Doorne.

I'm a recent graduate Multimedia and Creative Technologies at the Karel De Grote college in Antwerp. Growing up I always had an interest in videography and storytelling. This interest led me to study Multimedia and Creative Technologies. (Dutch course)

My eagerness to learn has helped me improve my skills and my ability to work well in team and take charge if need be. During my study I have enjoyed working on several projects from short films to documentaries to advertisements. Over the course of these projects, I have honed my skills as a cameraman, editor, grader and occasionally director.

In 2022 as part of my study I did an intership at RTV/TV Plus, where I was given the opportunity to work on one of their new programs, "Make-Over" among various other projects.

I have also gained experience through the occasional freelance project like Mind the Outfit and the more recent video for Mercedes TAC
