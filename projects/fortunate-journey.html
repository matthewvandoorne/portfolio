Fortunate Journey

A fortunate journey is a short-film made by a team of 19 students of the third year of Multimedia and Creative Technologies.
The short-film tells the story of a guy who is down on his luck. Through a dream he is confronted with the fact he needs to try to pick himself back up.

My work on this project was mainly centred around the "music video" part of his dream. For this part of the short-film I was the DOP, Grader and editor (alongside Yoran Bellens).
