Salmon Wrap

As part of an editing exercise for the subject Production Creator.
My group decided to make a video of someone making a salmon wrap.
The production was done in group and post-production had to be done individually.
The music choise was made during pre-production as we made our shotlist using the song.
During the production my role was that of director and cameraman.
