Mercedes TAC

2023

Because of my previous work with Mercedes TAC, I was asked to make a new video for them as they were looking for a new service advisor.

This video was made with relatively limited resources as it is my first freelance video.

The client and I first discussed what the feel of the video should be, besides the obvious focus on the job itself. As I had worked at TAC myself during summer holidays, I knew the company has quite a familial feel and suggested that it should be a focus of the video. With this in mind mr. Auman, Director of TAC, wrote his script while a prepared a shotlist.

The video itself was filmed with my own Sony A7III and recorded with a Rode Wireless Go II mic. And was edited in Premier Pro.

2020

One of the project I worked on during my study was a video for Mercedes TAC. This was filmed when garages where allowed to open again when the lock-down restrictions eased off. TAC wanted a video to show their customers that they where open again and that customers could safely bring their cars in for service or repairs.

My job in this project consisted of producing, directing and the camerawork. The editing was done by Christophe Verheyen and Prince Nziza.
Prince was also in charge of the audio, music and grading.
While Christophe was the gaffer on set and made the graphics.
